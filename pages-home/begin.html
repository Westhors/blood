iDonate

Save time at your donation!

Nebraska Community Blood Bank’s iDonate makes it easy and convenient for you to complete your health history questionnaire online on the day of your donation.  

Please note: All donors, visitors, and staff are required to wear a face mask or face covering. When presenting to donate, donors are asked to bring a face mask or face covering.
