While the world's attention is turning towards Britain, as its scientists continue their recent clinical trials about the hope vaccine against the Corona virus, a surprising statement came out from one of the world's leading immunologists, confirming that the virus will remain forever. In the details, Professor John Bell, a prominent scientist and professor of medicine at the University of Oxford, surprised the members of the British Parliament with a warning message that the Covid 19 virus may "remain endemic forever", indicating that the element that causes Corona infection may not be eliminated.

He also presented, during his speech in a session held by the "Health and Social Care Committee in the British House of Commons", evidence that any possible vaccine against the virus "is unlikely to have a sustained immune effect that will last for a very long time." "The indications are that we will witness rounds of vaccines and waves of disease proliferation, then more vaccines and more diseases," he added, addressing the parliamentary committee chaired by Jeremy Hunt, a former health minister and representative of the Conservative Party.
