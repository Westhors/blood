Experienced leadership, worldwide partnerships, and a legacy of saving lives

Nebraska Community Blood Bank, a division of Innovative Blood Resources, has been saving and sustaining lives since 1968. We are dedicated to connecting people who need life-saving blood with generous volunteers who give. Operating 4 donor centers and conducting dozens of blood drives each month, we are proud to be an independent nonprofit that supplies over 20 area hospitals and clinics with life-saving blood—from our community, for our community.
